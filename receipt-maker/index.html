Create receipts after payment

Doclira helps you create receipt PDFs on Android after a client has paid. Add the client, describe the service or item, review the amount, export a PDF, and share it with the client.

Useful receipt details

  • Client name
  • Service or item description
  • Amount paid
  • Date
  • Notes that help both sides remember the work

Keep your document trail organized

Receipts are useful when a client wants confirmation or when you want a simple local record of paid work. Doclira also supports paid and unpaid status tracking for practical follow-up.
