## Create PDF invoices from your phone

Doclira helps service businesses create practical PDF invoices on Android. Add a client, add service or item lines, review the total, export a PDF, and share it through WhatsApp, email, or any compatible app in the Android share sheet.

## What you can include

- Client and business details
- Service or item line descriptions
- Quantity and price
- Notes for the job or document
- Paid or unpaid status for follow-up
- PDF export for sharing and record keeping

## When it helps

An Android invoice maker is useful when you finish work at a client site and want to send a clear document before the details are forgotten. It is also useful for repeat clients because saved clients and saved services reduce repeated typing.
