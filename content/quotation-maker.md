## Send clear quote PDFs from Android

Doclira can be used as a quote maker for small service jobs. Use it when you want to describe proposed work, line items, quantities, prices, and notes in a PDF that is easy for the client to keep.

## What to keep clear

- The service being quoted
- Quantity and price for each line
- Any practical notes about the job
- Client name and contact details
- Your business details

## Keep quotes practical

A quote should be easy to read later. Use clear service descriptions instead of vague wording, then save and share the PDF through a compatible Android app.
