## Built for everyday service documents

Doclira focuses on the documents small service businesses need most: invoices, estimates, quotes, and receipts. The app is designed for quick use from an Android phone, especially when work happens away from a desk.

## Core features

- Create invoices, estimates, quotes, and receipts.
- Export documents as PDFs.
- Share PDFs through WhatsApp, email, or the Android share sheet.
- Save clients for repeat work.
- Save services or items to reduce typing.
- Track paid and unpaid documents.
- Work offline.
- Use the basic workflow without login.

## Why offline matters

Many service jobs happen in places with weak connectivity. Doclira lets you prepare documents on your Android phone, then share them when a connection is available.

## Designed for practical clarity

The best document is one a client can understand quickly. Doclira keeps the workflow focused on client details, line items, totals, notes, PDF export, and sharing.
