## Field documents for repair and installation work

Electricians and plumbers often need to list labor, parts, materials, and service notes. Doclira gives you a simple Android workflow for creating a PDF document at the job site.

## Useful for

- Repair visits
- Installation jobs
- Material line items
- Labor charges
- Estimate and quote requests

## Keep clients informed

Clear line items make the PDF easier for the client to understand later. Add practical notes, preview the document, then share it through a compatible Android app.
