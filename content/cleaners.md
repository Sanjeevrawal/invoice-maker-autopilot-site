## Cleaning invoices and estimates on Android

Cleaning businesses can use Doclira to prepare estimates before a job, invoices after a service, and receipts after payment.

## Useful for

- Deep cleaning jobs
- Recurring cleaning services
- Move-in or move-out cleaning
- Add-on services
- Client follow-up

## Practical document flow

Add the client, list each cleaning service clearly, review the total, export the PDF, and share it through WhatsApp, email, or another compatible Android app.
