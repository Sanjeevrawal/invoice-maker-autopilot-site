## Get Doclira on Android

Doclira is available on Google Play for Android users who need a simple mobile workflow for invoices, estimates, quotes, and receipts.

[Open Doclira on Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.doclira.invoice)

## Before you download

- Use Doclira to create invoices, estimates, quotes, and receipts.
- Export documents as PDFs.
- Share PDFs through WhatsApp, email, or the Android share sheet.
- Work offline when connectivity is weak.
- Use the basic workflow without login.

## Good fit for

Contractors, handymen, cleaners, electricians, plumbers, mobile mechanics, freelancers, tutors, photographers, and small service businesses that need documents while working away from a desk.
