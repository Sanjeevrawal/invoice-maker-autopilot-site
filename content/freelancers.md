## Simple invoices for freelance work

Freelancers need clear documents for projects, hourly work, sessions, and completed tasks. Doclira helps you create PDF invoices and receipts from Android and share them with clients.

## Useful line items

- Project milestone
- Hourly service
- Consultation
- Design or editing work
- Session or class

## Keep the workflow light

Use saved clients and saved services for repeat work, then export and share the PDF when the document is ready.
