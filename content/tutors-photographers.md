## Client documents for appointment-based work

Tutors and photographers often need simple documents for sessions, packages, and repeat clients. Doclira helps create PDF invoices, estimates, quotes, and receipts from Android.

## Useful for

- Tutoring sessions
- Photography packages
- Repeat clients
- Event or project quotes
- Receipt PDFs after payment
