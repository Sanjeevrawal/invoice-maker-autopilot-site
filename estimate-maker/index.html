Prepare estimates before the work begins

Doclira gives small service businesses a simple way to create estimates on Android. Add the client, describe the proposed work, enter service lines, preview the amount, and share a PDF copy when the client needs a clear document.

Estimate workflow

  1. Choose estimate as the document type.
  2. Add or select the client.
  3. Add service lines with quantities and prices.
  4. Add notes about the proposed work.
  5. Export the estimate as a PDF.
  6. Share it through WhatsApp, email, or the Android share sheet.

Who uses it

Contractors, cleaners, electricians, plumbers, mechanics, photographers, tutors, and freelancers can use estimates when the client wants pricing before approval.
